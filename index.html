Ingredients:

  • 1 lb lean ground mutton
  • 50 gr fat from lamb tail, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt to taste
  • Skewers or chop sticks

Preperation :

Leave the crushed red pepper in 1/2 cup of water
for about 2-3 hours, to get them softened. Then drain.

Mix all the ingredients with your hands.
Grab a fist size mixture and skewer it.
Squeeze it on the skewer until it gets smooth and
lengthens to acquire the shape shown in the picture.
Cook on a grill or oven. You can grill
some vegetables as well on the side.
Refrigerate the skewered Adana Kebab
overnight to get a better flavor.
